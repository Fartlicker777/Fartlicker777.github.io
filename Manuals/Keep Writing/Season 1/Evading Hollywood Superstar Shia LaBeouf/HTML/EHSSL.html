Strangling Superstar Shia Labeouf

  • You are now 1v1ing Shia Labeouf. When this happens, you will see a controller and a display pop up.
  • Shia Labeouf's move (not the name, but his button inputs) will be shown on the display.
  • Whenever Shia Labeouf does a move, you have seven seconds to input the corresponding retaliation move. After five correct moves, you will have killed Shia Labeouf.
  • For clarification: The correct retaliation move is the one in the same row. A and B refer to buttons that say A and B, while ULDR refer to directional buttons.
