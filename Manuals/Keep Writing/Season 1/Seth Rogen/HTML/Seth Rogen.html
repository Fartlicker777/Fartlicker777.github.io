Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod S.E.T.h Rogen

On the Subject of S.E.T.h Rogen

Modules based on Seth Rogen, Canadian movie actor, seem to be a thing now.

  • Characters from movies featuring, produced by, or written by Seth Rogen will be present on nine cards. These sets are NOT mutually exclusive, because every self absorbed director stars in his own movie.
  • Find the unique triplet that share the following rules and select them:
    • The movie they are all either within the same row (see below on how to make rows and columns) or different rows.
    • The movie they are all either within the same column or different columns.
    • Seth Rogen either has the same role in the movies, or has all different roles for the movies.
    • The movies either all have the same color background (seen directly below) or different color backgrounds.

  • To make the grid start with the first non-zero digit of the serial number and put the movie that corresponds to that in the top left cell of a 3x3 grid.
    • If you are an absolute zero, use 1 instead.
  • Remove that movie from the list above and use the next serial number digit (wrapping around if necessary) and put it in the next cell in reading order.
    • If you are still an absolute zero, continue to use 1 instead.
  • Start at the beginning of the serial number when you reach the end.
