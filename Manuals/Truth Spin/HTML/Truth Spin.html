On the Subject of Truth Spin

There is no truth, but rather just a perception of reality.

  • The screen above the three wheels will show a logic expression, however the logic symbols have been replaced with question marks.
  • On each of the three wheels, there will be three logic symbols, one at each cardinal direction.
  • Pressing each wheel will spin that wheel clockwise, as well as some other wheel counter-clockwise.
  • When the submit button is pressed, four equations are calculated: one for each cardinal direction. Each direction's symbols replace the questions marks in the equation (The symbols going from the inner wheel outwards, and replacing the question marks left to right.)
  • If the equation evaluates to true after the question marks are replaced, then the module will disarm.
