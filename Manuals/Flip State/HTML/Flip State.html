On the Subject of Flip State

"Oh I'm a top, I'm a bottom, I'm a switch."
You're twelve go do your Of Mice and Men reading assignment.

  • Take note of the state of each switch, and flip any switch to start the timer.
  • Use the table on the next page to determine which switch you should flip.
  • For each flip, use the rule corresponding to the LED that lit up in reading order.
  • Consider the switches to wrap around, where going left from the leftmost switch would take you to the rightmost one and vice versa.
  • You must also avoid flipping the switches into any of the possible states below.
  • Each flip after the first will have a maximum of 13 seconds for the next flip to occur. Failure to flip the correct switch in time will incur a strike, resetting the module.

Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Flip State
Stage 1:
  1. Flip the switch directly left of the one you flipped last.
  2. Flip the switch directly right of the one you flipped last.
  3. Flip the switch you flipped last.
  4. Flip the third switch.
  5. Flip the fifth switch.
 Stage 2:
  1. Flip any switch that has not been flipped yet.
  2. Flip either the second or the fourth switch.
  3. Flip the switch you flipped to initiate the timer.
  4. Flip any switch.
  5. Flip the switch right of the switch whose LED was lit last stage.
Stage 3:
  1. Flip any switch that has had the most flips so far.
  2. Flip any switch that has had the least flips so far.
  3. Flip the switch you flipped on stage 1.
  4. Flip any switch that has not been flipped yet.
  5. Flip any switch that points up.
 Stage 4:
  1. Flip any switch that did not point up during an odd stage.
  2. Flip either switch that is adjacent to the switch you flipped in stage 2.
  3. Flip the first switch.
  4. Flip any switch whose LED has lit up the most.
  5. Flip the switch right of the switch whose LED was lit in stage 1.
Stage 5:
  1. Flip any switch whose LED has not lit up prior to this stage.
  2. Flip any switch that has been flipped exactly once.
  3. Flip any switch in between the switch whose LED lit up in stage 1 and the switch flipped in stage 2.
  4. Flip any switch that has never pointed down.
  5. Flip any switch who direction is different from its neighbors.
 Stage 6:
  1. Flip any switch that points the same direction of the current direction of the switch you flipped in stage 3.
  2. Apply the stage 4 rule corresponding to the last flipped switch.
  3. Flip either the switch that is left of the switch you flipped in stage 3 or the switch right of the one whose LED lit up stage 4.
  4. Flip the switch that you flipped last.
  5. Flip the switch right of the switch that is right of the switch you flipped in stage 1.
Stage 7:
  1. Flip the switch whose LED has lit up the most.
  2. Flip the switch that has had the most flips.
  3. Flip the switch that has never been flipped.
  4. Flip the switch whose LED never lit up.
  5. Flip the fifth switch.
