Deaf's Completed Max Modes

As the title entails, all of the FNAF max modes (fangames included) I have completed and my thoughts on them.
If you don't give a shit about what I have to say, the playlist is here lol.

Name & Video Completion Game Name & Link Thoughts on Mode Personal Enjoyment Personal Difficulty MML Rating Date of Completion
Parasitic Pals Playtime with Percy This mode fucks and everyone should do it. Best fangame I've played, SCP has lost that title. 10/10 9/10 84 Dec 11, 2024
50/20 Ultimate Custom Night Despite the heavy RNG I had for doing the beginner strat, it was really fun to learn and pull off. I did have to do it twice because I forgot to record the first time, which was hella annoying. A lot of people say this is underrated on the MML and at this point idunno if it is. Honestly, if you know what you're doing, it's not that bad. You just have to learn the constituent parts first, and then piece them all together; piecing them together is where the difficulty lies. I'd say RNG was the cause of maybe 55% of my deaths, but that's mostly RNG within the first like 30 seconds so it doesn't matter lol. 8/10 9/10 92 Dec 1, 2024
7/20 FNAC, But Better! Also a fun max mode, and I think it's a bit underrated (or the others I've done are a bit overrated) on the MML, as most max modes take me around 2 hours to beat, this being no exception. 7/10 5/10 142 Oct 30, 2024
Rabbit Rumble Bonnie Custom Night Really fun max mode, but tbh it's very easy and I'm surprised it made it onto the MML website, even with how low it is. It only took me an hour after installing to beat it. 7/10 2/10 148 Oct 3, 2024
Golden Freddy FNAF 2 I have a rule about not doing max modes that are heavily RNG (ignore UCN lol), and this mode was the reason it took so long for me to touch the original max modes. Then I was made aware of the legendary brandon strat, though I hate using timers since it's not synced up with the game perfectly. It was a nice kinda zen experience, since it wasn't really hard to do, I just had to roll some good rng with certain cases and toy bitchhead. 6/10 4/10 N/A Aug 26, 2024
4/20 FNAF 4 This game is pretty mid tbh, and this mode annoyed the shit out of me since the hard part was nightmare himself, and getting to that took forever. I don't plan on doing the other like 4 max modes this game has to offer. 4/10 5/10 N/A Aug 21, 2024
Aggressive Nightmare FNAF 3 Shit game shit mode, FGP strat the goat. 3/10 3/10 N/A Jul 18, 2024
No Pain No Gain Extreme Five Nights at Wario's McDonald's 2 I genuinely can't believe this is a real thing. 7/10 5/10 N/A (used to be on the list lol) May 21, 2024
Worst Time Ever The Backdoor Project This is one of those rare fangames where it's of incredible quality and enjoyment, and is one of my favorite fangames I've ever played (also introduced me to Doors, so there's that). I originally laid eyes on it because I watched Hotel Hell's completion and I have some considerations on trying Hotel Hell itself, though it will definitely be a bitch to complete. 9/10 8/10 113 May 16, 2024
4/20 FNAF It's actually comical how much better the cam 2B strat is than the foxy cove strat. 5/10 1/10 N/A Nov 22, 2023
8/20 SCP: The Endurance This is my favorite fangame. Max mode itself isn't that difficult tbh. 10/10 6/10 120 Dec 1, 2022
Golden Freddy Very Hard FNAF: Sister Location I talked about how things are underrated and overrated, this mode is VERY much underrated. The cylindrical surface that the cursor lies upon makes Bonnet so difficult, combined with flipping because of Yendo because muscle memory is a bitch, I think the mode needs to go up quite a bit. Really fun to do though since every run is winnable, and every loss is entirely your fault. 8/10 8/10 130 Feb 24, 2022
